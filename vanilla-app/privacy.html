01 Information we collect

We aim to collect as little as possible. The information associated with your use of Virtus falls into a few categories:

Information you provide

  • Account details — such as your name, email address, and password if you choose to create an account or sync across devices.
  • Habit data — the habits you create, their icons, colors, attributes, schedules, completions, streaks, quests, and identity statements.
  • Support communications — messages you send us, including any details you choose to share.

Information collected automatically

  • Device & usage data — device model, operating system version, app version, and anonymized interaction events used to improve the experience.
  • Diagnostics — crash reports and performance logs that help us fix bugs.

By default, your habit data can be stored locally on your device. Cloud sync and backup are optional features you enable.

02 How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide core functionality — tracking habits, streaks, XP, quests, attributes, and achievements.
  • Sync and back up your data across your devices when you opt in.
  • Send reminders and notifications you have enabled.
  • Diagnose problems, prevent abuse, and improve performance and features.
  • Respond to your support requests.
  • Process subscription payments for Virtus Premium (handled by the App Store).

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use your habit data for advertising.

03 Where your data lives

Habit data is stored on your device and, if you enable sync, on secure cloud infrastructure operated by our hosting providers. We rely on industry-standard providers and store data only as long as needed to deliver the service.

04 Data security

We use technical and organizational safeguards — including encryption in transit, access controls, and regular review — to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security, but we work hard to protect your data.

05 Sharing & disclosure

We only share information in limited circumstances:

  • Service providers who process data on our behalf (e.g., cloud hosting, analytics, crash reporting) under confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal requirements when we are required to comply with applicable law or valid legal process.
  • Business transfers in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, with notice to you.

06 Third-party services

Virtus may use third-party services such as Apple's App Store (for purchases and subscriptions), cloud storage, analytics, and crash-reporting tools. These providers handle data under their own privacy policies. Payments are processed by Apple; we do not receive or store your full payment details.

07 Your rights & choices

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal information, and to object to or restrict certain processing. You can:

  • Edit or delete habits and identity statements at any time within the app.
  • Disable notifications and cloud sync in settings.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at [email protected].

08 Children's privacy

Virtus is not directed to children under 9 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us and we will delete it.

09 Data retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service. When you delete your account, we delete or anonymize your personal data within a reasonable period, except where retention is required by law.

10 Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date and, where appropriate, notify you in the app. Your continued use of Virtus after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
